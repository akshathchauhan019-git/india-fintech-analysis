# India Fintech Analysis 🇮🇳

A data-driven deep dive into India's UPI ecosystem across 9 years of growth (2016–2025), built as pre-joining research ahead of my Operations Associate role at Stripe's Service Delivery Centre, Bengaluru.

## 📊 Key Findings
- UPI hit 18.26 billion transactions in March 2025 (~200,000x growth from launch)
- Merchant payments (P2M) crossed 50% of UPI volume in 2024 for the first time
- India's digital payments penetration grew at 21% CAGR — fastest of any major economy
- 390 million merchant QR codes active — larger than global card POS terminals combined

## 📁 What's Covered
- Transaction volume & value trends (2016–2025)
- Market share breakdown (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, others)
- Fintech funding landscape
- UPI penetration vs GDP correlation
- Global expansion of UPI (UAE, Singapore, France, etc.)
